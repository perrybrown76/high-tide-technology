When, Not If

The question is no longer if a company will be breached — but when. High Tide Technology has designed a suite of insurance industry-specific cybersecurity services to address the NAIC Cyber Security Principles and protect the sensitive consumer data entrusted to insurers.

For Immediate Release — Las Vegas, June 8, 2015

“We have assembled a team of cybersecurity professionals with deep experience in the insurance industry to deliver services specifically designed to help insurance companies address each of the twelve NAIC principles. Our goal is to be a trusted partner in helping the insurance industry strengthen its cybersecurity posture.”

— Norman Chandler, President, High Tide Technology
