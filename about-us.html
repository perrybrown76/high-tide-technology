About High Tide Technology LLC

High Tide has been providing cost-efficient technical and professional services personnel to clients since 2006.

Experienced HT people have delivered customer solutions in the area of development, telecommunications, project management, data center operations, training, information technology and asset management, digital forensics, incident response, disaster recovery planning and information assurance audits.

These professional resources come from an active resume database of over 1,000 resources, most of whom have an average of 15 years' experience in their functional area.

HT maintains long term employee and customer relationships, and strives to treat employees and clients with the utmost respect.

Additionally, HT is headquartered in Montgomery, AL; we have successfully grown by establishing a reputation for offering superior customer service and for being financially sound.

Our business is built on the principles of Honesty and Integrity, Respect for the Individual and Responsiveness to Customer Needs.

In addition to providing people, HT has the ability to develop and manage customized business solutions to address a client's needs, pull together a team of experienced personnel to execute the project and administer the project through our internal billing and operational systems.

Additionally, our IT audit abilities and incident response teams are among the best-recognized in the nation.

HT offers end-to-end 'professional solutions for industry' by focusing on our core competency, people, thus allowing our clients to focus on the success of their business.
