Partnership

Ketch

High Tide is a Ketch partner. Ketch is a leading data privacy and consent management platform that helps organizations responsibly collect, control, and activate data while meeting global privacy requirements.

Ketch empowers businesses to navigate the complex landscape of data privacy regulations while maximizing the value of their data through responsible collection and activation practices.

Consent and Preference Management

Collect and manage user consent and preferences across all digital touchpoints.

Data Mapping and Discovery

Automatically discover and map personal data across your entire infrastructure.

Privacy Compliance Support

Comprehensive support for GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and other global privacy regulations.

Data Subject Request (DSR) Management

Efficiently process and fulfill data subject access, deletion, and portability requests.

Tag and Data Governance

Control how data is collected and shared through intelligent tag governance.

Privacy-Centric Data Activation

Activate consented data responsibly while respecting user privacy choices.

Integration with Marketing and Analytics Platforms

Seamlessly connect with your existing marketing and analytics tools.

As a Ketch partner, High Tide helps organizations implement robust privacy programs that build consumer trust, ensure regulatory compliance, and enable responsible data-driven growth.
