Partnership

Qualys

Being a Qualys partner allows High Tide to offer its clients a comprehensive suite of cloud-based security and compliance services through the Qualys Cloud Platform.

The Qualys Cloud Platform provides a unified approach to IT security and compliance, enabling organizations to discover, assess, prioritize, and remediate vulnerabilities and misconfigurations across their entire hybrid IT environment.

Vulnerability Management

Continuous vulnerability scanning and assessment across your entire environment.

Asset Discovery and Inventory

Automatically discover and catalog all IT assets across your organization.

Continuous Security Monitoring

Real-time monitoring for changes that introduce security risks.

Patch Management and Remediation

Streamlined patch deployment and vulnerability remediation workflows.

Configuration and Policy Compliance

Assess and enforce configuration standards and security policies.

Web Application Scanning

Automated scanning to identify vulnerabilities in web applications.

Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR)

Real-time endpoint visibility with threat detection and response capabilities.

Cloud Security and Cloud Asset Management

Secure and manage assets across multi-cloud and hybrid environments.

Threat Detection and Prioritization

Identify and prioritize threats based on real-world risk and exploitability.

Reporting and Compliance Management

Comprehensive reporting for regulatory compliance and security governance.

As a Qualys partner, High Tide helps clients implement proactive, scalable security solutions that provide continuous visibility, reduce risk, and maintain compliance across their entire digital infrastructure.
