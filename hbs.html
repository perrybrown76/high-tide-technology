Partnership

Heartland Business Systems

Being a Heartland Business Systems (HBS) partner allows High Tide to offer its clients advanced cybersecurity and risk management services designed to identify, validate, and reduce real-world security threats.

HBS delivers comprehensive security assessment and testing services that help organizations understand their actual security posture and take actionable steps to reduce risk.

Penetration Testing

Comprehensive network, application, and infrastructure penetration testing to identify exploitable vulnerabilities.

Vulnerability Identification and Validation

Discover and validate security vulnerabilities across your entire environment.

Security Risk Assessment and Analysis

Thorough assessment of organizational security risks with prioritized findings.

Threat Simulation and Exploit Testing

Simulate real-world attack scenarios to test your defenses under pressure.

Actionable Remediation Guidance and Reporting

Detailed reports with clear, actionable steps to address identified vulnerabilities.

Regulatory and Compliance Support

Ensure security assessments align with industry regulations and standards.

Ongoing Security Posture Evaluation

Continuous monitoring and periodic reassessment to maintain strong defenses.

As an HBS partner, High Tide helps clients take a proactive approach to security — identifying and addressing threats before they can be exploited, and building stronger, more resilient security postures.
