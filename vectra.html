Vectra

Being a Vectra partner allows High Tide to deliver AI-driven cybersecurity services for detecting, investigating, and responding to threats across hybrid and cloud environments.

Vectra uses AI-driven behavioral detection to find and stop cyberattacks that bypass traditional security controls, providing unmatched visibility into attacker behaviors across enterprise networks and cloud environments.

AI-Powered Network Detection and Response (NDR)

Machine learning-driven network traffic analysis to detect sophisticated threats.

Threat Detection for On-Prem, Cloud, and Hybrid Environments

Unified threat detection across all deployment models and infrastructure types.

Behavioral Analytics to Identify Hidden and Active Threats

Detect attacker behaviors that evade signature-based security tools.

Advanced Threat Hunting and Investigation

Tools and workflows for proactive threat hunting and deep investigation.

Automated Alert Prioritization and Triage

AI-driven prioritization that reduces alert fatigue and focuses analyst attention.

Integration with Existing Security Tools and SIEM Platforms

Seamless integration with your existing security stack and SIEM solutions.

Continuous Monitoring of East-West and North-South Traffic

Full visibility into both lateral movement and external communication patterns.

Incident Response Support and Remediation Guidance

Expert guidance for responding to and remediating detected security incidents.

Reduced Dwell Time Through Early Threat Identification

Detect threats early in the attack lifecycle to minimize damage and exposure.

As a Vectra partner, High Tide helps clients deploy and optimize AI-driven network detection and response — providing unmatched visibility into attacker behaviors and dramatically reducing the time to detect and respond to threats.
